ITEM NAME: Bone Chips

Classic Era (added 1999-2000)


Bone Chips


WT: 0.1  Size: SMALL
Class: ALL
Race: ALL


Bone Chips are used as a spell reagent by Necromancers and Shadow Knights to summon their pets. All such spells require two chips except Cavorting Bones which requires one.
Bone Chips are also used in numerous quests for low level players to gain experience up to level 4 or so, the same quests may be used by higher level players for faction.
Being accessible to low level players and time-consuming to farm by high level players, there will always be an economy for Bone Chips, giving low level players access to quick cash.
