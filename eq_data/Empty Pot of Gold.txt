ITEM NAME: Empty Pot of Gold

Velious Era (added December 2000-2001)


Empty Pot of Gold


LORE ITEM  NO DROP
WT: 0.0  Weight Reduction: 0%
Capacity: 2  Size Capacity: GIANT


 Item Lore (from Identify): Its magic may be able to create gold from magical items
Container used to create class-specific Plane of Mischief armor, obtained from Bob the Painter. 
Each combine uses one Armor of Distraction piece and a class-appropriate doll:

 A Wily Warlock Doll (Wizard)
 Sly Summoner Doll (Mag)
 Mad Monkey Doll (Monk)
 Crazy Cleric Doll (Cle)
 Maleficent Necromancer Doll (Nec)
 Mischievous Dazzler Doll (Enc)
 Shamanistic Shenannigan Doll (Shm)
 Twisted Druid Doll (Dru)

Can also be used to make Funny Money by combining Words of Wealth and one Armor of Distraction piece.
