 Item Lore (from Identify): Axe of the Chosen
This axe is widely considered to be the best two-hander in the game for listed classes. It has a spectacular ratio, unbeatable tank stats, and useful lifetap proc. A warrior with this axe may forego dedicated rampage-tanking weapons for it, as well as use it for tanking dangerous proccing enemies such as Venril Sathir, a dracoliche and Maestro of Rancor.
