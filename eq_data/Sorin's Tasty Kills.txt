Hot Cross Buns
Just like Grandma used to make. Hot Cross Buns are a good, old-fashioned, gritty delight. Char up some of these anytime.
Ingredients:
Clump of Dough, Fruit, Frosting
Cooking Instructions:
Mix a clump of dough with fruit and divide into 12 equal parts. Roll each part into a ball and bake at high hear until blackened and crispy, cool.
Make an X mark with frosting on each bun.
Tastes great with:
Elven Veal and Ale
