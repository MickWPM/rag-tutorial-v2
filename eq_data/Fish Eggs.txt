ITEM NAME: Fish Eggs

Classic Era (added 1999-2000)

Fish Eggs


WT: 0.1  Size: SMALL
Class: ALL
Race: ALL


To make Fish Eggs, combine either a Thunder Salmon or Greengill Salmon and a Skinning Knife in a Tackle Box (Trivial Fishing 135). It will produce 2 Fish Eggs and 2 Fish Scales.
