AC: 2

 Item Lore (from Identify): Comical Currency... DUH!
If you give Peachy D`Vicci 2x Funny Money you will receive a random colored Gift Box in return.
Make sure you turn in the money unstacked and take care that this quest can not be multiquested.
To make funny money, you have to combine one Words of Wealth with an Armor of Distraction in the Pot of Gold.
You receive one funny money per combine.
